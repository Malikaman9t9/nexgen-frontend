Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SEO Audit Tool.

Why is an SEO Audit important for my website?
An SEO audit is like a health checkup for your website. It helps identify technical roadblocks that prevent search engines like Google from crawling and indexing your pages. By fixing these issues, you improve your site's visibility, organic traffic, and ultimately, your revenue.
Is NexGenWebLab really free to use?
Yes! Our Starter plan is 100% free forever. It allows you to run technical SEO checks, analyze Core Web Vitals, and get basic AI suggestions. For agencies and power users needing Bulk Analysis and Live Traffic Data, we offer an affordable Enterprise Pro plan.
How does the AI Action Plan work?
Our engine utilizes advanced AI models to read your specific audit errors. Instead of giving generic advice, it formulates a tailored, step-by-step strategy telling you exactly what code to change or what content to optimize to fix the issue.
