SEO Audit Insights & FAQs

Frequently asked questions about Technical SEO and Reporting.

What is included in a Technical SEO Audit?
A technical SEO audit includes a comprehensive check of your website's health. NexGenWebLab analyzes meta tags, header hierarchy (H1-H6), SSL security, XML sitemaps, canonical tags, server response times, and content-to-code ratios to ensure your site is perfectly optimized for search engines.
How does the White Label SEO Report Generator work?
Available in our Enterprise Pro plan, this tool allows agencies to remove NexGenWebLab branding and replace it with their own logo, colors, and company information. The seo report generator then creates a professional PDF or Word document that you can present directly to your clients.
Why are Core Web Vitals critical for ranking?
Core Web Vitals are a set of metrics that Google uses to measure user experience (speed, responsiveness, and visual stability). A poor core web vitals test score can lead to lower rankings. Our tool provides specific AI-driven advice on how to improve your LCP, CLS, and TBT scores.
