Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about our technical SEO auditing tool, white label reports, and billing.

What is included in the free SEO audit?
Our free seo audit scans your primary domain for critical technical roadblocks. It includes a comprehensive check of meta tags, header structures, image ALT text, SSL security, and basic mobile responsiveness.
How does the White Label SEO Report feature work?
Available exclusively on the Enterprise Pro plan, our seo report generator allows agencies to strip all NexGenWebLab branding from the final export and apply custom agency logos and colors.
Do you monitor Core Web Vitals?
Absolutely. Every technical seo audit executes a live core web vitals test using Google's Lighthouse API, measuring LCP, CLS, and TBT for both mobile and desktop.
Can I audit multiple websites at once?
Yes, Pro users have access to our bulk seo checker. Upload an Excel (.xlsx) file containing hundreds of client or competitor URLs for simultaneous processing.
What makes the AI Action Plan different?
Standard tools list errors. Our AI reads your specific site's context and provides tailored solutions — like having a senior SEO expert guiding your team step-by-step.
